How it works

Pricing clarity in three steps

1
Enter your ZIP or state
We pull the local median costs for your area from verified, source-cited surveys — not facility-submitted estimates.
2
Select care type and needs level
Costs vary widely by care type (assisted living vs. memory care vs. home care) and by the level of daily assistance required.
3
Get a realistic cost range
See the median, 25th percentile, and 75th percentile — with monthly and annual projections so you can plan finances clearly.
4
Understand Medicaid coverage
Each state page explains what Medicaid does and does not cover for each care type, with links to state benefit programs.
Data sourcing

Where the numbers come from

Every figure on Carepriced is cited. We do not use facility-submitted estimates or sponsored pricing data.

Genworth / CareScout Cost of Care Survey 2025
The industry's most comprehensive annual pricing survey, covering 440 metro markets across all 50 states.
Primary source
NIC MAP Senior Housing Data
Quarterly data on occupancy rates, rental rates, and supply for 140 primary and secondary markets.
Primary source
CMS Cost Reports (Form 2540-24)
Federal reporting data from Medicare- and Medicaid-certified skilled nursing facilities nationwide.
Primary source
State Medicaid Rate Schedules
Published reimbursement rates for HCBS waiver programs and nursing facility Medicaid bed rates by state.
