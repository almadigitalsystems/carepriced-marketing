What this estimate covers

  • Base monthly rate (rent + meals + standard care services)
  • Care-intensity adjustment (additional ADL support, memory care, mobility assistance)
  • Room-type adjustment (shared / studio / one-bedroom)
  • State cost-of-living factor (BLS Regional Price Parities)

It does not include: one-time community fees, medication management add-ons, transportation packages, or facility-specific upcharges. Carepriced's facility-direct pricing rows (rolling out post-launch) will surface these on a per-operator basis.

Methodology and sources

  • National median: $5,511 / month for assisted living, Genworth Cost of Care Survey 2024 Annual Report.
  • State adjustment: New York all-items Regional Price Parity = 116.5 (where U.S. = 100), Bureau of Economic Analysis 2022 release.
  • Care intensity: Low 0.85× / Medium 1.00× / High 1.30× (industry-standard ADL-load adjustment).
  • Room type: Shared 0.75× / Studio 1.00× / One-bedroom 1.25× (industry-standard living-arrangement adjustment).
