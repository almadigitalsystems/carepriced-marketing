Care types we cover

Assisted Living

National median ~$5,350/mo. Help with daily activities, meals, social programming.

Memory Care

20–50% above assisted living. Secure environment for dementia and Alzheimer's care.

Independent Living

National median ~$3,000/mo. 55+ communities with amenities, no daily care.

Nursing Home

National median ~$8,800/mo for semi-private. 24/7 skilled medical care.

How we source pricing

  • Genworth/CareScout Cost of Care Survey 2025 (state and metro medians)
  • NIC MAP Senior Housing data (context only)
  • CMS Form 2540-24 (skilled nursing comparator)
  • Operator-direct rate cards from Carepriced founding-partner facilities (where available)

About these estimates

Medians are starting points, not quotes. Actual cost depends on care level, room type (private, shared, studio), and add-ons such as medication management, physical therapy, and 24/7 nursing. Memory care typically runs 20–50% above assisted living for the same facility. State pages break down sub-area pricing for major metros where data is available.
